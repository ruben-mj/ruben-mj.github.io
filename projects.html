Due to Covid-19 many non-profits had to cease their actions in order to comply to the measures set by the Belgian government. A local team of volunteers in my town organise an annual fair-trade breakfast to support Broederlijk Delen's actions in the South. Covid-19 was still raging in our area and gatherings of 5+ people were illegal. The breakfast, usually held in a big hall accommodating +-100 people, was not an option. By setting up a simple webshop for them using mijnwebwinkel I was able to set up, automate and transform their breakfast into a takeaway breakfast.
They are considering opening the takeaway annually as an additional option.

Since the start of 2020 I've been active on my neighbourhoods Hoplr. Via Hoplr I provide accessible and affordable digital support for anyone who needs it. During the Covid period many preferred at-home service. It usually involves elderly people having small digital struggles. This mostly includes but is not limited to:

  • Fixing printers
  • Fixing internet connectivity
  • Configuration problems
  • Hardware problems
  • Game consoles
  • Purchases
  • Custom PC building

More advanced support is also possible. People wanting to open a website, improve their social media activities and looking to market their business have approached me. Even though marketing isn't the main purpose of my service, I was still able to help them create and publish several successful adverts. I also provide full social media and website maintenance for a small monthly payment.
